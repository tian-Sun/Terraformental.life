About This Game

Bru & Boegie: Get da MILK! is a hand-drawn 2D surrealist comedy point-and-click adventure game. You play as Bru, a strange stoic man-child on a mission: get milk for da coffee. Point and click your way through a fully hand-drawn and animated world, featuring a fully-voiced cast and an original soundtrack.

Key Features

  • One-Button Gameplay
  • Interactions deluxe
  • Rarefied Humour
  • Original Hand-Drawn Art & Animation
  • Fully-Human Voiced Cast
  • Original Music Score
  • Mini-Games

Controls

  • Left-click: Interact / Use
  • Right-click: Examine
  • Esc: Pause / Options
  • F: Flash Hotspots
  • D: Dab (after you learn it)
  • Tab: Skip Cutscene
  • 1-9: Dialogue Options
